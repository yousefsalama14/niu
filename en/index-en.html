Why study at Al-Nahda University!

Commitment to honesty, ethical principles, credibility and fairness in everything the university does.

Integrated curriculum

To provide balanced and integrated study programs based on the principles of ethics and responsive to the sciences of the times.

Abundant research and scientific products

Providing scientific and educational research and books useful to the community by university students and professors.

Accept any student

We accept learners of both sexes without restriction of age or nationality.

Distance education system

The distance learning system makes it easier for students to seek knowledge without being in direct attendance.

University certificate

The university is equivalent to the certificates of the institutes of preparation of Islamic preachers.

Authentication of certificates

The possibility of documenting certificates from the Embassy and foreign affairs at the student's own expense.
