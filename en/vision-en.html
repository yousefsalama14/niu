University vision

To contribute real and effective to the introduction of new visions for the development of distance education, by building a scientific edifice and a distinctive knowledge that spreads the culture of peace and love, in an atmosphere of human values, scientific research, and ensures the quality of educational outputs in accordance with the standards of quality and mastery, and provides the labor market with distinguished pioneers.

University's Mission

Nurturing creativity and innovation, and taking care of pioneers and leaders by providing a climate of faith and higher values, in parallel with the scientific atmosphere based on objectivity and careful specialization.

To facilitate the request for shariah, human, educational and Arabic studies for those who find it difficult to achieve formal education because of its limitations
