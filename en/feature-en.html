University Features

We accept learners of both sexes without restriction of age or nationality.

The distance learning system makes it easier for students to seek knowledge without being in direct attendance.

Study in the Language of the Holy Quran (the Arabic language).

The university is equivalent to the certificates of the institutes of preparation of Islamic preachers.

We issue our certificate in Arabic, Turkish and English.

Available to answer your queries 24/7.

The university works by equating published and unpublished literature.

The possibility of documenting certificates from the Embassy and foreign affairs at the student's own expense.

University Values

Integrity

Commitment to honesty, ethical principles, credibility and fairness in everything the university does.

Excellence

Seeking optimum performance in everything the University does as a distinct and purposeful institution.

Diversity

Promoting acceptance of difference and respect for others.

Accountability

Commitment to loyalty and goodwill in the university community.

Respect

Treating others with dignity.

Social responsibility

contributing to the intellectual, cultural, spiritual and economic progress of society.

Creativity

Promoting innovation and critical thinking for personal skills development.
